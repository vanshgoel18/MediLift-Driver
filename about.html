ABOUT

In today’s traffic world, ambulance plays a major role when an accident occurs on the road network and the need arises to save valuable human life. Transportation of a patient to an emergency hospital seems quite simple but it is quite difficult and gets more difficult during peak hours.
In our Ambulance Booking System, people can easily book an ambulance. There are two major modules namely User, and Ambulance. Users can register and log in using credentials. Users can edit their profile and change their password in an emergency. Any Upcoming Ambulance Booking details if anyone wants to Book an Ambulance or if there is an Emergency.
For booking an ambulance users must select ambulance size, pick-up point & hospital, and date & time. In an emergency we will automatically book the nearest ambulance. Users will get a list of All the bookings of Ambulances.
